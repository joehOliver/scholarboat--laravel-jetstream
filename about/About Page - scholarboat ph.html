About Us

ScholarboatPH is a website born from the initiative of Grade 12 students from Philippine Science High School - SOCCSKSARGEN Region Campus to bridge the gap on attainable education by providing relevant information on a variety of scholarships. It is a tool that hopes to expand the students' perspective of education.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inform Filipino students about the different scholarship opportunities that they may avail in the Philippines through an online scholarship directory. We aim to relay such information so that the Filipino youth may be able to pursue quality college education despite financial barriers.

Our Vision

We envision a future with more attainable college education for the Filipino youth.
